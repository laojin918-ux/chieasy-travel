1. Purpose

This Refund Policy governs cancellations, refunds, booking modifications, chargebacks, and all payment-related matters concerning services provided by Chieasy Travel.

By making any payment to the Company, the Client acknowledges and accepts this Refund Policy in full.

2. Nature of Services

2.1. Chieasy Travel primarily provides:

  • travel planning;
  • destination consulting;
  • travel coordination;
  • itinerary development;
  • organizational support;
  • travel hosting;
  • supplier sourcing;
  • travel assistance.

2.2. The Client acknowledges that a substantial portion of the Company's work is performed before the actual commencement of travel.

2.3. The value of the Company's services derives primarily from professional expertise, planning, consulting, coordination, destination knowledge, and organizational efforts.

2.4. Such services may be fully or partially performed prior to travel.

3. Non-Refundable Professional Services

3.1. Once delivered, any of the following services shall be considered fully performed and non-refundable:

  • custom itineraries;
  • route planning;
  • travel strategies;
  • destination consultations;
  • supplier recommendations;
  • travel roadmaps;
  • travel planning documents;
  • personalized travel materials;
  • travel support documentation;
  • itinerary revisions requested by the Client.

3.2. The Client acknowledges that these services constitute intellectual and professional work specifically prepared for the Client.

3.3. The Company shall retain all fees relating to such completed services.

4. Cancellation Before Work Commences

4.1. If the Client cancels before any work has commenced, the Company may refund payments received.

4.2. Payment processing fees, banking charges, currency conversion fees, and transfer costs may be deducted.

5. Cancellation After Work Commences

5.1. Once the Company has started performing services, refunds shall be reduced by:

  • completed work;
  • administrative costs;
  • consulting services;
  • planning services;
  • supplier sourcing services;
  • operational expenses.

5.2. The Company shall determine the extent of completed services based on internal records and communications.

6. Confirmed Bookings

6.1. Once third-party bookings have been confirmed, refunds shall be reduced by:

  • non-refundable supplier charges;
  • cancellation penalties;
  • booking fees;
  • processing fees;
  • planning fees;
  • operational expenses.

6.2. The Company shall not be required to reimburse amounts that are not refunded by suppliers.

7. Client Cancellation More Than 30 Days Before Departure

7.1. The Company may retain:

  • all completed consulting services;
  • all completed planning services;
  • all non-refundable supplier costs;
  • administrative expenses.

7.2. Remaining amounts may be refunded at the Company's discretion after applicable deductions.

8. Client Cancellation Between 30 and 15 Days Before Departure

8.1. The Company may retain up to 50% of organizational service fees.

8.2. Additional supplier charges and non-refundable costs shall also be deducted.

9. Client Cancellation Between 14 and 8 Days Before Departure

9.1. The Company may retain up to 75% of organizational service fees.

9.2. Additional supplier costs shall remain payable by the Client.

10. Client Cancellation Within 7 Days of Departure

10.1. All organizational, consulting, planning, coordination, and hosting services shall be deemed fully performed.

10.2. No refund shall be required except where legally mandated.

10.3. Any recoverable supplier refunds may be forwarded to the Client after receipt.

11. No-Shows

11.1. Failure to appear for a travel experience constitutes a no-show.

11.2. No refunds shall be provided for no-shows.

11.3. All services shall be deemed fully performed.

12. Early Termination of Travel

12.1. If the Client voluntarily leaves a trip after commencement:

  • unused days;
  • unused activities;
  • missed experiences;
  • unused accommodations;
  • transportation segments;

shall not automatically generate any refund entitlement.

13. Missed Services

13.1. No refunds shall be granted for services missed due to:

  • late arrival;
  • missed flights;
  • visa problems;
  • denied entry;
  • lost travel documents;
  • illness;
  • failure to follow instructions;
  • Client negligence.

14. Third-Party Suppliers

14.1. Airlines, hotels, transport providers, activity operators, guides, and other suppliers maintain their own cancellation policies.

14.2. The Client accepts that supplier policies may be stricter than this Refund Policy.

14.3. The Company shall not compensate the Client for supplier decisions.

15. Force Majeure

15.1. Refunds during force majeure situations shall be limited to amounts actually recovered from suppliers.

15.2. Force majeure includes:

  • natural disasters;
  • epidemics;
  • pandemics;
  • war;
  • terrorism;
  • civil unrest;
  • government restrictions;
  • border closures;
  • transportation shutdowns;
  • emergencies.

15.3. The Company shall not be required to compensate losses resulting from force majeure.

16. Subjective Dissatisfaction

16.1. Refunds shall not be granted due to:

  • weather;
  • crowds;
  • queues;
  • local conditions;
  • personal preferences;
  • cultural differences;
  • language barriers;
  • subjective dissatisfaction;
  • unmet personal expectations.

16.2. Travel experiences are inherently subjective.

17. Limitation of Refunds

17.1. Under no circumstances shall the Company be required to refund more than the amount actually received and retained by the Company for its own services.

17.2. Third-party supplier costs shall be excluded from refund calculations.

17.3. The Company shall not be responsible for indirect or consequential losses.

18. Chargebacks

18.1. The Client agrees not to initiate unjustified chargebacks.

18.2. In any payment dispute, the Company may rely upon:

  • booking records;
  • invoices;
  • itineraries;
  • emails;
  • messages;
  • photographs;
  • videos;
  • travel records;
  • supplier confirmations.

18.3. Any attempt to recover funds through payment disputes after services have been performed may be challenged by the Company.

19. Refund Process

19.1. Refund requests must be submitted in writing.

19.2. The Company may request supporting documentation.

19.3. Approved refunds shall be processed within a reasonable period after calculation and verification.

20. Final Provisions

20.1. This Refund Policy forms an integral part of the Company's Terms & Conditions.

20.2. The Company reserves the right to amend this Policy at any time.

20.3. Updated versions become effective upon publication.

20.4. This Policy shall be governed by the laws of Portugal.
